About Undead Corridor

If you enjoy intense survival horror experiences, Undead Corridor is a game you shouldn’t miss. This thrilling browser-based title throws players into a dark, zombie-infested hallway where quick thinking and fast reflexes are the keys to survival. Whether you're playing casually or aiming to beat your high score, Undead Corridor delivers a gripping and adrenaline-filled experience.

What Is Undead Corridor?

Undead Corridor is an online action-survival game where players must navigate through a narrow corridor filled with relentless waves of undead enemies. The objective is to survive as long as possible while avoiding or eliminating zombies that approach from all directions.

The game combines elements of endless runner and shooter mechanics, making it both accessible and challenging for players of all skill levels.

Gameplay Overview

The core gameplay focuses on movement, aiming, and survival. Players must constantly stay alert as zombies appear suddenly, forcing quick reactions to dodge or attack.

Key Controls:

  • Arrow keys / WASD: Move character
  • Mouse / Spacebar: Attack or shoot

Objective:

  • Survive as long as possible
  • Eliminate as many zombies as you can
  • Achieve the highest score
